Featured Issues

WDI's multi-faceted approach addresses various aspects of gender inequality and strives to create a more equitable and inclusive world.

Economics
Women Economic Empowerment

WDI is committed to enhancing economic opportunities for women. This service includes initiatives to improve women's access to education and vocational training, promote women entrepreneurs and business leaders, and support women in accessing financial resources and markets. WDI aims to reduce economic disparities and empower women to achieve financial independence.

Gender
Promoting Gender Equity and Social Inclusion

WDI works towards creating a more inclusive society where all individuals, regardless of their gender, have equal opportunities and rights. This service involves advocacy, awareness campaigns, and programs aimed at breaking down gender-based barriers and biases. WDI promotes diversity and inclusion in various sectors, fostering an equitable society.

Governance
Advancing Gender Governance and Democracy

WDI advocates for improved gender representation and participation in governance and decision-making processes. This service involves collaborating with policymakers, conducting research, and promoting policies that promote gender-responsive governance. WDI aims to ensure that women's voices are heard and considered in matters of governance and democracy.

Campaign
Campaign for Women/Gender Environmental and Climate Justice

WDI recognizes the unique impact of environmental and climate issues on women. This service focuses on advocating for gender-responsive environmental policies, addressing climate change's disproportionate effects on women, and empowering women to become environmental leaders and advocates. WDI aims to protect women's rights in the face of environmental challenges.

Justice
Enhancing Gender Access to Justice and the Rule of Law

WDI is dedicated to ensuring that women have equal access to justice and legal protection. This service involves legal advocacy, providing legal assistance to women facing gender-based violence, and working to reform legal systems to be more gender-sensitive. WDI aims to address barriers to justice and ensure that women's rights are upheld.

WDI prioritizes women's health and reproductive rights. Women in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) and Digital Space" is a broad and multidimensional concept that encompasses the participation, challenges, and contributions of women in STEM disciplines and the digital landscape. Data
Women Evidence-Based Data

WDI recognizes the importance of data and digital platforms in advancing gender equality and women's empowerment. This service focuses on harnessing data and digital technology to drive evidence-based policies and initiatives that benefit women. It involves collecting and analyzing gender-disaggregated data to identify disparities and inform decision-making. Additionally, WDI utilizes digital spaces, such as social media and online platforms, to amplify women's voices, share resources, and facilitate discussions on gender-related issues.
