Careers

Regardless of your position within the Women Democratic Institute, every day presents an
opportunity for you to apply your skills, talents, and knowledge to make a meaningful impact
in the lives of women and contribute to positive change in the world.

Women Democratic Institute believes that a Gender Equal World is possible as long as we
assemble the dedicated, passionate, and winning team working towards just, equitable and
sustainable world. Together we can make this vison reality.

Alternative Ways to Join WDI

Internships

At Women Democratic Institute, we open our doors to aspiring students pursuing post-high school education,
offering them a unique chance to dive in, gain valuable insights, and contribute to our
mission of empowering women.
Through our internship program, you'll collaborate closely with our dedicated team, actively working
towards enhancing the lives of women. Here, you'll not only acquire practical work experience but
also immerse yourself in a world of learning and growth. Join Us

Volunteer Opportunities

Do you have a strong passion for supporting women in realizing their dreams and aspirations?
Are you interested in offering your services pro bono? At Women Democratic Institute (WDI),
we provide a platform where your volunteer contributions can truly make a difference.
Whether you're looking for one-time opportunities or seeking to establish ongoing partnerships,
you have the power to create meaningful change! Start Now
