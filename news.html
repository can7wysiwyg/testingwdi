Join WDI for the 1st Malawi Women of Influence Award 2023

The Women Democratic Institute (WDI) is delighted to announce the upcoming
"Malawi Women of Influence Award" Project, an event dedicated to honoring and celebrating the remarkable achievements
of influential women in Malawi.

Date: October 19, 2023

Venue: BICC Lilongwe, Malawi

This initiative resonates deeply with WDI's mission to promote gender equality, empower
women, and drive positive change in society. We invite you to join us in recognizing
and celebrating the outstanding contributions of these inspirational women who have
made significant impacts in their respective fields. Event Highlights

  • Inspiring Stories: Hear the powerful stories and achievements of Malawi's influential women.
  • Award Ceremony: Witness the prestigious awards presented to these exceptional leaders
  • Networking: Connect with like-minded individuals passionate about gender equality.
  • Advocacy: Learn how you can contribute to the cause of empowering women and promoting gender equity in Malawi.
